## Airbnb rating calibration
- Title: The Illusion of 5 Stars: Cross-City Rating Calibration and Visibility Disparities on Airbnb
- Venue framing: CS 281 Ethics of AI · Spring 2026 · Stanford
- Uses Inside Airbnb data from Berlin, Chicago, Hong Kong, and Tokyo
- Asks whether identical star ratings carry equivalent meaning across cities and whether calibration differences translate into visibility disparities
- Key public findings include: common thresholds (e.g. 4.8 / 4.9) correspond to different local percentile positions; gaps persist in matched cohorts and OLS controls; review language differs by city at matching stars; re-ranking by within-city percentile changes the top tier substantially (~40% of raw top 1% remain after city-normalization)
- Poster: /media/research/airbnb-rating-calibration-poster.pdf
- Portfolio room: /research/airbnb-rating-calibration (and /work/airbnb if linked)

## Poem-to-Song
- Title: The Sound of a Sonnet: Poem-to-Song Recommendation with Pseudo-Supervision
- Venue framing: CS 229 Machine Learning · Spring 2026 · Stanford
- Collaborators listed: Cheney Sang, Amelia Sarah Bloom
- Dual-encoder / contrastive learning over poems and song lyrics with pseudo-supervised labels (no labeled poem–song dataset)
- Corpus framing: thousands of poems (PoetryDB) and song lyrics (Genius / Spotify metadata)
- Best feature-aware encoders reported ~78–80% agreement with human triplet judgments (human-human agreement ~80% as ceiling); MPNet-only baseline ~67%
- Models leaning on emotional tone and structure outperform literal word overlap
- Poster: /media/research/poem-to-song-poster.pdf
- Portfolio room: /research/poem-to-song

## POMDP aid allocation
- Title: Budget-Constrained Aid Allocation as a POMDP
- Venue framing: Decision Making Under Uncertainty · Spring 2026 · Stanford
- Collaborator listed: Abi Lopez
- Models food and housing assistance allocation under partial observability and a shared budget; solved with PBVI vs baselines
- Documented takeaway: even optimal planning hits environmental limits when housing recovery is structurally slow
- Paper PDF: /media/research/pomdp-aid-allocation-paper.pdf
- Portfolio room: /research/pomdp-aid-allocation
